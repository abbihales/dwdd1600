Prince Edwards Island

Price Range Between $700-$1,000 for 5 days.

Prince Edward Island is a picturesque escape, known for its stunning red sand beaches, rolling green hills, and charming coastal villages. Visitors can explore the iconic Anne of Green Gables sites, savor fresh seafood like lobster and mussels, or enjoy outdoor adventures such as cycling the Confederation Trail. With its warm hospitality and tranquil beauty, PEI offers the perfect getaway for relaxation, family fun, and cultural discovery.
